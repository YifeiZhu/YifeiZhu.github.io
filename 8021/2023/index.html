Objectives

This is a half of the graduate compulsory courses in Geometry and Topology, the other half being MAT8024 Differentiable Manifolds. The main topics are covering maps and homology.

For undergraduate students, this is a sequel to MA323 Topology, developing further algebraic (and computable) machinery beyond the fundamental group to analyze spaces qualitatively.

Prerequisites

Topology (MA323) or consent of the department.

Textbooks

We will be working from Chapters 5 to 13 of John M. Lee's Introduction to topological manifolds, second edition, briefly reviewing compact surfaces and the fundamental group.

Useful references include Allen Hatcher's Algebraic topology, Haynes Miller's Lectures on algebraic topology, and 姜伯驹《同调论》.
